About Zhambyl Region

Zhambyl region is located in the south of Kazakhstan, and it is famous for sacred places, historical and cultural sights. There is an opportunity to walk
along mountain gorges, sand dunes or go on a hiking photo hunt.But the main attractions of the Zhambyl region are, of course,
sacred and historical objects.

Attractions In Zhambyl

Koksay

The Koksay gorge is an icon of the Zhambyl region. It is also located in a nature reserve and is the object of organized sightseeing tours.

Zhambyl Region

Mausoleums

From a historical point of view, the region is rich, therefore, here you can find many mausoleums, each of which has its own beautiful, sometimes sad legend that has come down from time immemorial, for example, the mausoleums of Aisha-bibi and Babadzhi-khatun, Karakhan, Dautbek, and Shokay datka.

South Kazakhstan, Zhambyl Region

Akmeshit Cave

The Akmeshit cave beckons with its mystery and attracts scientists and tourists from all over the republic. Nobody knows how this cave appeared because for millions of years it was closed, that is,it had no connection with the outside world, but in the 20th century, part of it collapsed and mankind got a chance to get inside.
