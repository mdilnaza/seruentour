About Karaganda Region

Karaganda is a region in the central part of Kazakhstan, located in the very center of the Eurasia continent.
It is the largest in terms of territory and industrial potential, rich in minerals and raw materials.

Attractions In Karaganda

Balkash

Balkhash is an endless semi-freshwater lake in the southeastern part of Kazakhstan, the second largest salt lake and 13th place in the list of the largest lakes in the world. The uniqueness of the lake lies in the fact that it is divided by a narrow strait into two parts with different chemical characteristics of the water - in the western part it is practically fresh, and in the eastern part, it is salty. In the Karaganda part of the lake, there is an infrastructure and a resort town where you can spend your time interestingly
