About East Kazkhstan

East Kazakhstan is a kind of unique region where you can observe all kinds of landscapes, such as snow-capped mountains, green forests,
sandy deserts and endless steppes, through which nomads roamed. In these large natural variations, perhaps the mountains are the reason
why people come to discover this place. The mountains of the East Kazakhstan region attract with their beauty, power and unique view,
which can be captured after climbing a high point.

Attractions In East Kazakhstan

Katon-Karagay

This is the largest national park in Kazakhstan, protected by UNESCO. There are waterfalls, coniferous forests, healing lakes and health resorts such as "Rakhmanovskiye Kluchi". It's located at an altitude of 1770 m above sea level, surrounded by picturesque lakes containing medicinal radon (a decay product of radioactive elements). This element is called medicinal for humans and is used to treat conditions such as pain in the spine, joints, and skin..
