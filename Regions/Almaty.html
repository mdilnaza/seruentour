About Almaty

Almaty is shrouded in mountains from all sides, sunny, green, with modern and historically important buildings, an incredibly beautiful city.
It is worth noting that here the rich history of Kazakhstan in the 20th century is reflected. There are many attractions in the city,
such as Medeu, Park of 28 Panfilov Guardsmen, museums, theaters, Kok Tobe, etc.
Furthermore, in honor of the city of Almaty the authorities gave the name to the region that surrounds it.
There is the nature behind which many Kazakhstanis and foreign tourists visit each year. Big Almaty Lake with a beautiful landscape
and crystal clear water,the world's second largest grand-canyon named "Charyn Canyon" will not leave anyone indifferent.

Attractions In Almaty

Shymbulak

Shymbulak is a popular ski resort in Kazakhstan, located in the Trans-Ili Alatau. If you want to plunge into the winter atmosphere and ride a steep mountain, then come there, we repeat, you will not regret it.

Kerey and Zhanibek Khans St 558/1, Almaty 050020

Charyn Canyon

There are only two grand canyons all over the world, the first is in the USA, and the second is in the Almaty region of Kazakhstan. The Charyn Canyon has formed about 26 million years ago on the site of a large lake.It has many breathtaking attractions and one of them is the Valley of Castles,where you can find stones of various unusual shapes, reminiscent of castle towers.

Ile-Alatau National Park

Ile-Alatau National Park is the most prominent place in Almaty Region. During rest or excursions, here you can observe unique landscapes from the steppe to alpine meadows and peaks covered with eternal snow and glaciers.On the territory of the national park, there are unique natural monuments such as spruce forests, waterfalls, alpine lakes, stone glaciers, and glaciers, as well as objects of cultural and historical heritage.This national park is suitable for people who are bored with city life and want to explore new places. Additionally, breathing fresh air, drinking stream water, and getting away from digital life for a moment could significantly increase happiness and boost health.

Abay Ave 64, Almaty

How to get there Getting Around
  • By Plane

    Air Astana is the national air carrier of Kazakhstan, based in Almaty.
    It operates regular international and domestic flights on 64 routes from its main hub, Almaty International Airport,
    and from a secondary hub, Nursultan Nazarbayev International Airport.

  • By Train

    If you are already in another city of Kazakhstan, you can get to Almaty by train.
    You will arrive in Almaty 1, or to Almaty 2,
    it's closer to downtown from which you can get to any part.

  • By Bus

    There are plenty of bus routes for tourists to go anywhere for cheap price in the city of Almaty.
    Today, fare payment is available with a transport card, as well as by means of a QR code.
    There are the following mobile applications for this: Transcard, Kaspi.kz,
    SMSBUS, Homebank, My Activ, My Beeline, Senim, Rakhmet, Astra Bus, Plus24 (Kassa24), Qiwi and OYBY

  • On foot

    A walk allows you to immerse yourself in the pleasure of traveling from one point to another.
    You will get to know the city you are visiting better, besides, it's possible to talk with the locals to get acquainted with Kazakh culture, see amazing artistic architecture and just enjoy!
